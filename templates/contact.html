Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cave Mapper?

Cave Mapper is an online platform dedicated to providing comprehensive information, resources, and a community for cave explorers, enthusiasts, and researchers worldwide. Our mission is to promote the discovery, documentation, and conservation of caves while fostering a passionate community of cavers.

How can I contribute to Cave Mapper?

There are several ways you can contribute to Cave Mapper. You can submit cave data and maps, share your caving experiences and photos, engage in discussions on our community forums, and support our conservation efforts. We welcome contributions from cavers of all skill levels and backgrounds.

Is there a mobile app for Cave Mapper?

Yes, we have mobile apps available for both iOS and Android devices. The Cave Mapper mobile app allows you to access cave information, record your caving trips, and connect with the community on the go. You can download the app from the App Store or Google Play Store.

How can I ensure my safety while caving?

Caving can be a challenging and potentially dangerous activity. It is essential to prioritize safety by receiving proper training, using appropriate gear, and following established guidelines. Cave Mapper provides resources and information on cave safety, but we always recommend seeking guidance from experienced cavers and joining reputable caving organizations.
