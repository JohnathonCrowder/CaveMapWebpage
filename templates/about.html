About Cave Mapper

Cave Mapper is the ultimate platform for cave enthusiasts, explorers, and adventurers. Our mission is to create the most comprehensive, accurate, and user-friendly cave database in the world, empowering people to discover, learn about, and experience the incredible underground landscapes that lie beneath our feet.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a team of passionate cavers and technology experts, Cave Mapper was born out of a shared desire to revolutionize the way people explore and understand caves. With decades of combined experience in caving, cartography, and software development, we set out to create a platform that would not only provide reliable, up-to-date information about caves but also foster a vibrant community of like-minded individuals.

Since our inception, we have worked tirelessly to build the most extensive cave database in existence, partnering with caving organizations, government agencies, and individual explorers from around the world. Today, Cave Mapper is proud to feature over 50,000 caves across 120 countries, with new additions and updates being made every day.
